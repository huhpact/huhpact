I handle the full path from concept to shipped product: brand and visual design, marketing assets and campaign planning, and the actual frontend build. Most clients bring me in either for a single piece of that chain or for the whole thing end to end, whichever fits the project better.
Small design or marketing pieces usually land within a few days. A full site build with design and frontend included typically takes two to four weeks depending on scope and how much content is ready on your side. I'll always give a real estimate after a short call rather than a generic number upfront.
Pricing depends on scope, so I quote per project rather than by the hour. As a rough anchor, standalone design work usually starts in the low four figures, and combined design-plus-build projects scale up from there. Reach out with the details and I'll send a proper quote, no obligation.
